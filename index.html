Built around your flight

One card captures everything — automatically.

ADS-B Auto-import
Enter your registration and Volio pulls your route, speed, altitude and duration automatically — no manual logging ever.
Route Map
Every flight plotted on a live map with departure and arrival pins and the exact track you flew.
Your Story
Add a title and a personal note to every flight — give your adventure the context it deserves.
Likes & Sharing
Pilots react to each other's flights and share them with one tap — building a real aviation community.

How does it work?

1
Fly

Via the ADS-B implementation, after your flight just look up your registration number!

2
Share

On the feed all other users can see the activity. Add a title, a comment and show it to the others!

3
Inspire

Let your friends and colleagues know about your last flight. It could help them plan their next adventure!
